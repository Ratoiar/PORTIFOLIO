Starry Nights

An Enchanting Sparkle Script

This magical script adds twinkling stars to your webpage, bringing enchantment to your digital world. You can customize their number, size, color, and twinkling frequency, creating a galaxy-like effect on your screen. Make the stars bigger in the JS to see that they're actually shaped like stars.
